About Me

Hello, my name is Megan Cho. I am a sophomore studying Linguistics and Computer Science with a minor in Film at University of California, Los Angeles. I was born in Dusseldorf, Germany, and I have lived in other countries including Canada, Australia, and the U.S. ever since I left Germany. I am currently residing in New Jersey, and I love to visit home over school breaks.

During my free time, I like to read, play video games, watch movies, and listen to music. My favorite movie director is Martin Scorsese, and I could probably name all of his films up-to-date! I also like to watch and play sports. I am a Brooklyn Nets fan, and am very excited for when Kevin Durant will play again next season.
